# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

At Heliora, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Because Heliora is a decentralized protocol built on the Solana blockchain, our data handling practices differ from traditional centralized platforms.

## 1. Information We Collect

### On-Chain Data
When you interact with the Heliora protocol, we collect information that is publicly available on the Solana blockchain. This includes:
- Your public wallet address.
- Transaction history related to the Heliora program.
- Position data and liquidity contributions.

### Off-Chain Data
We do not require you to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or physical address to use the protocol. However, we may collect:
- **Usage Data**: Non-identifying information about how you interact with our website (via self-hosted analytics).
- **Communication Data**: If you contact us for support, we may retain a record of that correspondence.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the Heliora protocol.
- Facilitate the creation and resolution of prediction markets.
- Monitor for protocol security and prevent fraudulent activity.

## 3. Data Sharing

Heliora does not sell your data. We only share information in the following circumstances:
- **Blockchain Transparency**: All transactions on the Heliora protocol are public by design.
- **Service Providers**: We may use third-party tools (like RPC providers or IPFS) to help deliver the service.
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of our users.

## 4. Your Rights

As a user of a decentralized protocol, you have the right to:
- Access your on-chain data via any Solana block explorer.
- Cease using the protocol at any time by disconnecting your wallet.

## 5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of the Heliora platform. However, the security of your funds is ultimately your responsibility. Always ensure you are using the official Heliora website and never share your private keys or seed phrases.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
