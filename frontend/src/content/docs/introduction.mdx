# What is Heliora?

Heliora is a next-generation prediction market protocol built on Solana, designed from the ground up to be **AI-Native**. Unlike traditional prediction markets which are simply AI-powered, Heliora uses autonomous agents as the core infrastructure for market creation, liquidity provision, and resolution.

<Callout type="tip">
  Heliora is currently live on Solana Devnet. Join our community to help shape the future of autonomous markets.
</Callout>

## The Problem

Existing prediction markets like Polymarket and Kalshi have paved the way for decentralized forecasting, but they suffer from significant limitations:

1.  **Polymarket**: Lacks a robust Agent SDK, making it difficult for developers to build autonomous trading or market-making bots.
2.  **Kalshi**: Operating as a regulated entity, it recently faced regulatory hurdles that led to the freezing of $77M in user funds, highlighting the risks of centralized or semi-centralized infrastructure.
3.  **Low Efficiency**: Traditional markets rely on human intervention for market creation and resolution, leading to high latency and limited market breadth.

## Why AI-Native?

Heliora is AI-native, meaning every step of the market lifecycle is orchestrated by specialized AI agents:

- **AI-Powered**: Uses AI as an optional tool (e.g., for sentiment analysis).
- **AI-Native**: AI is the protocol. The agents *are* the market participants and administrators.

### Core Value Proposition

- **Speed**: Markets are deployed in less than 400ms after a real-world event is detected.
- **24/7 Liquidity**: Autonomous market-making agents ensure tight spreads around the clock.
- **Trustless Resolution**: A 5-persona consensus system ensures objective and tamper-proof outcomes.
- **Developer First**: A comprehensive SDK allows anyone to build their own agents on top of Heliora.
